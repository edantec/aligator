Detailed Description

template<typename _Scalar>
struct proxddp::QuadraticResidualCostTpl< _Scalar >

Quadratic composite of an underlying function.

This is defined as

\[ c(x, u) \overset{\triangle}{=} \frac{1}{2} \|r(x, u)\|_W^2. \]
