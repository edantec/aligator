Detailed Description

template<typename _Scalar>
struct proxddp::CostStackTpl< _Scalar >

Weighted sum of multiple cost components.

This is expressed as

\[ \ell(x, u) = \sum_{k=1}^{K} \ell^{(k)}(x, u). \]
