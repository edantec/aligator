Detailed Description

template<typename _Scalar>
struct proxddp::TrajOptProblemTpl< _Scalar >

Shooting problem, consisting in a succession of nodes.

The problem can be written as a nonlinear program:

\[ \begin{aligned} \min_{\bfx,\bfu}~& \sum_{i=0}^{N-1} \ell_i(x_i, u_i) + \ell_N(x_N) \\ \subjectto & \varphi(x_i, u_i, x_{i+1}) = 0, \ i \in [ 0, N-1 ] \\ & g(x_i, u_i) \in \calC_i \end{aligned} \]
